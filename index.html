Just a little about me...

I am a full stack developer with a focus in Javascript in the Denver area. I am an avid outdoorsman and love taking advantage of everything that Colorado has to offer. I first became interested in web development through a desire to design my own website. I love the freedom and creativity that web design provides which sparked an interest into larger scale website functionality, and ultimately led to me enrolling in the Web Development Immersive program at Galvanize. Since graduating from the program, I have been continuing to learn new technologies through personal projects and freelance work. Feel free to browse through my portfolio which contains some of the projects I have worked on. Cheers.
